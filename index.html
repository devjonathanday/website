Hyperspace Collapse
Hyperspace Collapse is an endless first-person shooter where you, a stranded astronaut, must fight for survival in the void of space against a horde of alien robots.

This game was created as an entry for the AIE Battle for Train Jam, a 48 hour jam where teams competed for school sponsorship to attend Train Jam. The theme for the jam was "Limited Space."
Trial By Fire
Play as a pyromancer who is attempting to pass their trials and become a true master of the flame!

This game was created for AIE's minor production with an eight week development period.
Goo Fighter
Goo Fighter is a 2 player brawler game where you and a friend can duke it out in a best-of-3 arena tournament! Mash buttons and use your goo arms to slap your opponent and become the victor!

This game was made in 4 weeks for an assessment in game physics.
Gyro Fighter
Dodge buildings and shoot down enemies in this endless flyer!

This game was made in for an assessment in mobile game development.
