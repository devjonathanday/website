I began teaching myself to program in 2011 by making simple games in Game Maker. Later on, I attended a video game design course in high school for 2 years, which cemented my career goals in game development. I went on to attend another 2 year game programming course at Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AIE) in Seattle, WA. In 2020, I started working as a game developer professionally. I've always loved video games and will continue to pursue my dreams as a developer into the future.

Many of my projects are available to download for free. I attend game jams wherever possible, and I frequently collaborate with game artists and designers to create fun experiences. I am always learning new things along the way, bettering myself for the video game industry!

I mainly specialize in game programming, but I enjoy 3D modeling and composing music for personal projects as well.
